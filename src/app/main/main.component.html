About Carmel Public School

Carmel Public School is a renowned educational institution committed to providing quality education and nurturing young minds with a strong foundation in academics, values, and holistic development. Situated in the serene surroundings of Potma, Sabalpur, Suriya, Giridih, Jharkhand, the school fosters an environment where students are inspired to excel and evolve into responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals.

At Carmel, we believe in creating a balance between academic excellence and co-curricular activities, ensuring the all-round development of every student. With a dedicated team of educators, modern infrastructure, and state-of-the-art learning facilities, the school offers a conducive atmosphere for students to explore their potential and become future-ready.

  • Moral values: To build character and instill discipline.
  • Holistic development: Through sports, arts, and extracurricular activities.
  • Modern teaching methodologies: To enhance critical thinking and creativity.
  • Strong community engagement: Promoting values of empathy, respect, and responsibility.

Carmel Public School aims to nurture students with not only the knowledge to achieve academic success but also the life skills to face challenges with confidence, preparing them to contribute positively to society and make a meaningful impact in the world.
